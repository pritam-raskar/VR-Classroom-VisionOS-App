# Sanctions QA Digital Worker - Presentation Script
**Total Duration: ~3 minutes 30 seconds**


## SLIDE 1 - Title (~25 seconds)
Good morning everyone.

Today I'd like to introduce the Sanctions QA Digital Worker - an AI-powered solution we've built to transform
how we perform Quality Control on sanctions screening dispositions.

Let me walk you through the why, what, how, and impact.


## SLIDE 2 - Why We Need This (~45 seconds)

Today our QC process is entirely manual and Excel-based.
Analysts review dispositions one by one in spreadsheets - it's slow and hard to scale.

Now, new regulatory obligations require us to increase QC from 1% to 3% of daily transactions - a 3-fold increase.
With the current manual process, that's simply not manageable - it would require tripling analyst hours.

On top of that, Excel makes it difficult to allocate work and track progress across the team.

The opportunity is clear -
build a Digital Employee that handles this 3x volume, and gives us one single platform
to consolidate, allocate, and track all QC work.

## SLIDE 3 - What is the Digital Worker? (~40 seconds)

Think of it as an AI-powered QA analyst.
It reads the same data a human would -
transaction details, investigation notes, client communications - and evaluates whether the screener made the right call.

Three things to know:

First - AI-powered.
Specialized agents emulate how an analyst reads, reasons, and evaluates.

Second - human oversight.
Uncertain cases are always flagged for a human analyst.
The AI never makes a final call alone.

Third - every disposition gets a clear outcome:
AI reviewed, needs human review, or out of scope.

Operations subject matter experts provide the instructions - the AI follows their guidance.

---

## SLIDE 4 - How It Works (~40 seconds)

Four simple steps.

Data comes in daily and is automatically sampled.

Each sampled disposition goes through three AI agents - a Worker assesses, a Fixer validates,
and if they disagree, a Challenger breaks the tie.
This consensus approach ensures quality.

Results are categorized -
in-scope, needs human review, or out-of-scope - and everything is stored on dashboards for reporting.

Cases are also smartly routed by entity type - individual, company, vessel, or other - so each review uses the right lens.

## SLIDE 5 - Impact & Value (~35 seconds)

The numbers speak for themselves:
3x QC coverage - from 1% to 3% - without growing the team.
80% time saved on routine reviews.

For the business - scalability without headcount.

For analysts - less tedious spreadsheet work, more focus on cases that truly need human expertise.

For compliance - every AI review produces a structured, auditable rationale - meeting the new 3% mandate with a complete trail for regulators.

To summarize:
AI reviews and extracts.
