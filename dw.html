What is the Digital Worker?

A Digital Employee that uses AI to review sanctions screening dispositions — checking whether screeners made the right call on each transaction, and evaluating the quality of their investigation and documented evidence.

AI-Powered Review

Persona-based AI agents emulate how a QA analyst reads, reasons, and evaluates each sanctions disposition — at scale.

Human Oversight

Cases the AI is uncertain about are flagged for human analyst review — the AI never makes a final call alone on ambiguous cases.

Structured Output

Every review produces a documented rationale, stored in Snowflake and surfaced on dashboards — ready for audit.

How It Works

Click any step to learn more

Worker → Fixer → Challenger

Three AI agents review each disposition in sequence. The Worker makes the initial assessment, the Fixer verifies it, and if they disagree, the Challenger acts as the tiebreaker.

Smart Routing

Dispositions are intelligently routed based on rationale type — Individual, Company, Vessel, or Other — ensuring each review uses the right lens.

💰 Scalability Without Headcount

Tripling QC coverage from 1% to 3% of daily transactions no longer requires tripling the team. The Digital Worker handles the 3-fold increase in volume, freeing analysts to focus on complex cases that truly need human judgment.

📈 Operational Efficiency

What once took hours of spreadsheet work now runs automatically each day. Results are delivered to dashboards and stored in Snowflake — no more emailing Excel files.

💡 Less Tedious Work

Analysts no longer manually compare fields in spreadsheets. The Digital Worker extracts and compares data automatically, presenting clear findings and reasoning for each disposition reviewed.

🎯 Focus Where It Matters

Ambiguous cases are flagged for human review, so analysts spend their expertise on dispositions that truly need a human eye — not routine false positives.

🛡 Consistent & Auditable

Every AI review produces a structured rationale with reasoning. No more variability between analysts. Every decision is documented and traceable for audit purposes.

📋 Regulatory Readiness

Meeting the new 3% QC mandate is now achievable without scaling headcount. The complete audit trail — including AI reasoning and human overrides — demonstrates robust compliance to regulators.
