What is AurorAI?

An end-to-end AI platform that processes Bridger sanctions alerts through a multi-agent pipeline, delivering structured recommendations to analysts.

Multi-Agent AI Engine

5 specialized AI agents work in sequence — analyzing, deciding, and quality-checking each alert.

Oracle Persistence

Every decision, score, and rationale is persisted to Oracle — providing a complete audit trail for regulators.

Audit & Reporting

Complete decision trail in Oracle — every recommendation, confidence score, and action is traceable.

Maker-Checker Pattern

Maker proposes a decision; Checker (different AI model) independently validates. Disagreements flagged for human review.

Alert-Level Consolidation

One alert may have multiple hits. Consolidator aggregates all hit-level decisions into one alert-level recommendation.

The Maker-Checker pattern eliminates decision drift from fatigue, shift changes, or varying analyst experience. Two different AI models independently evaluate each alert, and disagreements are flagged for human review.
